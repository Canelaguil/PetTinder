What is PetMatch?

Sometimes all you need to do is look deep in their eyes... to know you've found your match. It's true for humans and pets both, but one sometimes forgets with the endless rows of pet pictures on shelter websites. That's why we are introducing PetTinder: a personal way to find a pet. Feel the connection with your potential companion and compare your favorites to find the perfect match. Everyone deserves the love of a pet, and so do you.
